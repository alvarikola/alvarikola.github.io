    Tyrannosaurus Rex

    Tyrannosaurus is a tyrannosaurid theropod dinosaur that lived in the late Cretaceous period, approximately 68 to 66 million years ago, in the Maastrichtian, in what is now North America.

    Carnotaurus

    Carnotaurus is a genus of theropod dinosaur that lived in South America during the Late Cretaceous period, probably between 71 and 69 million years ago.

    Velociraptor

    Velociraptor is a genus with two known species of dromaeosaurid theropod dinosaurs that lived towards the end of the Cretaceous period, 75 to 71 million years ago, during the Campanian period, in what is now Asia.

    Herbivores

    Triceratops

    Triceratops is a genus with two known species of ceratopsian ceratopsian dinosaurs, which lived at the end of the Cretaceous period, approximately 68 to 66 million years ago, during the Maastrichtian, in what is now North America.

    Parasaurolophus

    Parasaurolophus is a genus of hadrosaurid ornithopod dinosaurs, which lived at the end of the Cretaceous period, approximately 76 to 73 million years ago, in the Campanian, in what is now North America.

    Ankylosaurio

    Ankylosaurus magniventris is the only known species of the fossil genus Ankylosaurus of ankylosaurid thyreophoran dinosaurs, which lived in the late Cretaceous period, approximately 68 to 66 million years ago, during the Maastrichtian, in what is now North America​.

    Omnivores

    Troodon

    Troodon is an ancient wastebasket taxon and potentially dubious genus of relatively small, bird-like theropod dinosaurs definitely known from the Campanian era of the Late Cretaceous period.

    Therizinosaurus

    Therizinosaurus is a genus of very large therizinosaurid that lived in Asia during the Late Cretaceous period in what is now the Nemegt Formation about 72.1 million years ago to 66 million years ago.

    Oviraptor

    Oviraptor philoceratops is the only known species of the extinct Oviraptor genus of oviraptorid theropod dinosaur, which lived at the end of the Cretaceous period, approximately 75 to 71 million years ago, between the Campanian to Maastrichtian, in what is now Asia.
